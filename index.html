About

Hi! My name is Kyriaki but everyone calls me Korina.


I am an enthusiastic learning designer and passionate about blending technology with learning as well as helping learners build confidence in their educational journey. I am also a Linguistics graduate (MA with Honours) with a solid foundation in cognitive sciences and programming.


Overall, I like making stuff, find joy in being creative and have recently discovered my deep interest in web development.

I love taking photos wherever I go and express through my photos to share my view of the world. You can have a look at some of the photos I took over the years on my wordpress page.
